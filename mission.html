Our Mission / Objectives:

Our mission is to gear-up efforts in improving the health and livelihood of the people of River Cess County and it's environs by providing safe drinking water, progressive agriculture road map to nutrition, entrepreneurship, and improved sanitation facilities. Our ultimate goal is to fight against poverty, ill-health through inducing poverty reduction strategies, and sustainable development.
