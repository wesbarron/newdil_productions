Motto: Changing the world begins with thinking about another people's livelihood.




LIBERIA Present Image: Liberia is a West African country that is condensed with underpriviledged communities. Obviously, River Cess County is one of those communities in the country. Presently, Liberia is increasingly drowning in economic down-turn with devasted livelihood of citizens. Given the magnitude of the Liberian economic crisis, New Deal Image-Liberia was formed to put River Cess County and Liberia on the path of poverty reduction, especially marginalized people of the River Cess community.
